Who Sampled

DOMAIN : Music Application and an algorithm music game INTERVENTION : Add a feature to an existing app

PROJECT: Since 2012, Who Sampled is a platform that has a database of more that 100,000 artists They have information about sampled music or sample-based music, cover songs and remixes. To sum up It’s an easy way to track musical samples and cover songs.
